## Design Context

### Users
Primary visitors are recruiters, collaborators, founders, and technically literate peers evaluating MJ Kang's product judgment and range. They arrive to quickly understand professional credibility, scan proof of execution, and open a few selected projects without friction.

### Brand Personality
Thoughtful, rigorous, quietly ambitious. The tone should feel editorial rather than flashy: warm, precise, and confident without looking over-produced.

### Aesthetic Direction
Structured portfolio with an understated print-like grid, paper-toned background, and black-ink contrast. Motion should feel crisp and intentional, with subtle reveals and tactile feedback instead of decorative spectacle.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the first screen immediately legible; animation should guide attention, not compete with the content.
2. Preserve the editorial grid and warm palette while adding a stronger sense of polish and momentum.
3. Favor lightweight transform-and-opacity motion over heavy effects or new dependencies.
4. Treat project exploration as the main interactive surface and give it the clearest feedback.
5. Respect reduced-motion preferences by falling back to a fully usable static presentation.
